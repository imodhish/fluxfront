ACHIEVEMENTS

FIELD MANUAL

  • The Flux is a fluid. It pours from red Emitters, flows downhill, pools in low ground, and crushes anything it touches.
  • Everything is built by energy packets shipped from your Command Core across the network. No connection, no construction.
  • Collectors and Relays extend the network and are the only structures others can link through.
  • Cannons mow shallow Flux at the front line. Mortars crack deep pools at range. Both eat energy as ammo.
  • Reactors and Batteries grow your economy. A red flow rate means you are spending faster than you produce.
  • Win by charging a Nullifier next to every Emitter. It fires once, erases all Emitters in range, and is consumed.
  • X recycles structures for a refund. Height is your friend — build on ridges, defend the passes.
  • Structures need flat ground: the whole footprint must sit on one terrain level. Big buildings need big plateaus.
  • You can place structures beyond the network — they wait as dormant ghosts and come online once a connected Collector or Relay reaches them.
  • Drag in build mode to chain-build a whole line — Relays and Collectors auto-space to stay linked.
  • Relocate Cannons, Mortars and the Core: select one, then click it again and pick a flat destination. It flies there — offline (and the network is down while the Core is airborne). Nullifiers cannot move.
  • MOVE mode (G): drag a box over several weapons to grab them all, then click a destination — they fly there keeping their formation. RECYCLE mode (X) also box-selects: drag to scrap many, double-click to scrap a whole cluster of one type.
  • The Terp sculpts the land: select it, set a target level (−/+ or [ ]), then click or drag ground in range. Raising and lowering, one packet per level step. Walls, moats, platforms.
  • Collectors earn energy from the ground their field claims — fields never overlap, so spread them wide.
  • A nullified Emitter leaves a Power Zone: build on it for big boosts (reactor ×6, weapons ×2 fire rate + range, battery ×3, backbone reach ×1.5).
  • Ancient Relics sit on high ground. Hold a linked Collector or Relay near one for 12 s to claim a permanent buff.
  • Every minute or two the Flux surges — all Emitters ×2.5 for 8 s. The music swells 8 s before it hits. Listen.
  • Deep Flux erodes cliffs over time, and it never stops growing while Emitters live. No wall is forever — you are always on the clock.
  • Spore Towers lob airborne Flux blobs at your structures — walls can't stop them. Only Beams shoot spores down. Nullify the towers to win.
  • Sprayers blanket ground with friendly Anti-Flux that annihilates Flux on contact — a chemical wall for places terrain can't protect.
  • Emitters have personalities: Breeders spawn children, Migrants burrow away from charging Nullifiers, and the INSANE Boss shrugs off the first strike.
  • Supply priority (P / ⇄ button): BALANCED splits packets fairly between construction and ammo; WEAPONS or BUILD forces one lane first. The HUD SUPPLY row shows who's waiting (B = build, A = ammo). Bigger energy reserves ship packets faster.
  • Digitalis (dark red web) grows wherever Flux feeds it, lets Flux climb cliffs along it, and carries Runners that stun your structures — Snipers (N) pick them off.
  • Aether Totems channel to the FORGE while a linked backbone holds them: spend æ on permanent fire-rate, packet, power and damage upgrades.
  • Cryo Towers (C) flash-freeze Flux into raised ice ground for 25 s — bridge a flooded basin and rush a Nullifier across. Strafers (V) fly sorties against the deepest Flux nearby.
  • After any run: COPY REPLAY shares your whole game as a paste-able code; RACE GHOST replays your last attempt as blue outlines while you try to beat it. Mutators on the menu twist the rules.
  • Shields (B) push Flux away while powered — cover a Nullifier build. Converter (K) captures an Emitter to pump Anti-Flux for you. Bomber (J) carpet-bombs Anti-Flux.
  • Lose your Core and you get 25 s to redeploy a new one from orbit — click a flat landing site. Run out of time and the sector is lost.
  • Minimap (corner): click or drag to jump the camera. T shows every weapon range. Flux squalls rain Flux map-wide; Magma emitters turn Flux to rock.
  • Win runs set records & achievements (menu). COPY CHALLENGE shares your exact map+time; friends TAKE CHALLENGE to race it. R records a clip. Tune audio/visuals in SETTINGS.
  • Economy: Pylons speed packets across the network · Guppy Pads air-link distant outposts · build a Harvester on a purple Aether Node for extra Forge tech.
  • Support: Repair Bays mend nearby structures · Resonators overcharge adjacent weapons (but draw Spores) · Sensor Towers scout · Inhibitors slow Flux to a crawl in a zone · the Siphon sits ON deep Flux and drains it into energy.
  • Touch: tap to preview, tap again to confirm. Pinch (or scroll) to zoom, drag to pan.
  • Press ? anytime for a one-screen keyboard cheat-sheet.
