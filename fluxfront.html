FIELD MANUAL

  • The Flux is a fluid. It pours from red Emitters, flows downhill, pools in low ground, and crushes anything it touches.
  • Everything is built by energy packets shipped from your Command Core across the network. No connection, no construction.
  • Collectors and Relays extend the network and are the only structures others can link through.
  • Cannons mow shallow Flux at the front line. Mortars crack deep pools at range. Both eat energy as ammo.
  • Reactors and Batteries grow your economy. A red flow rate means you are spending faster than you produce.
  • Win by charging a Nullifier next to every Emitter. It fires once, erases all Emitters in range, and is consumed.
  • X recycles structures for a refund. Height is your friend — build on ridges, defend the passes.
